Tribute to MS Dhoni

Biography

  • Mahendra Singh Dhoni, born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, India, is a former Indian cricketer and captain of the Indian national team. Widely regarded as one of the greatest wicket-keeper batsmen in the history of cricket, Dhoni is known for his calm demeanor and exceptional leadership skills. He led India to victory in the 2007 ICC World Twenty20, the 2010 and 2016 Asia Cups, and the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup.

Major Achievements

  • ICC T20 World Cup Winner (2007)
  • ICC Cricket World Cup Winner (2011)
  • ICC Champions Trophy Winner (2013)
  • Most Successful Captain in IPL History with Chennai Super Kings
  • Order of Merit from the Government of India (Padma Bhushan)
